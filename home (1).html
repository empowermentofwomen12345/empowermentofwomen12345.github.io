Who We Are

WomenEmpower is a non-profit initiative supporting women’s education, leadership and entrepreneurship. Founded in Dhaka, we work to create sustainable growth for women through skills training, mentoring, and financial literacy.

Our Mission

To help women learn, lead, and live independently by providing education, confidence, and resources.

Our Vision

To build a future where every woman can reach her potential and contribute to a better society.
